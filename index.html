Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2026 · Last updated: April 29, 2026

SVLTMK LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ImportHelm mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information ImportHelm handles, where it is stored, and which third-party services we use to deliver the app's features. We aim to keep this document accurate and current with how the app actually behaves.

1. Information You Provide

ImportHelm is built around data you choose to enter:

  • Import-management data — products, HTS codes, suppliers, shipments, broker records, costs, refund claims, and any notes you create. By default this data is stored on your device.
  • Photos & documents — if you use the photo classification, document OCR, or container-camera features, images you capture are sent to our processing services to extract information (HTS suggestions, invoice fields, package dimensions). Images are not retained for training.
  • AI chat messages — questions you type into the in-app AI Chat are sent to our AI provider to generate the answer. Conversations are stored locally on your device.
  • Refund Finder uploads — CSV entry data you upload is sent to our analysis service to detect refund eligibility, then results are returned to your device.

What we do NOT collect: we do not request bank account numbers, credit card numbers, social security numbers, government IDs, or biometric data. We do not run advertising, ad tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Facebook SDK, Google Analytics).

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to deliver the features you trigger:

  • Calculating duties, landed costs, and refund estimates
  • Classifying products from photos or descriptions
  • Extracting fields from invoices, bills of lading, and packing lists
  • Tracking shipments, deadlines, and compliance status you enter
  • Providing intelligent answers in the AI Chat
  • Diagnosing crashes and stability issues to improve reliability

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to build profiles outside of delivering ImportHelm features.

3. Where Data Is Stored

The vast majority of your data lives on your device:

  • On-device (AsyncStorage) — products, shipments, refund claims, AI chat history, calculator inputs, and your settings. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.
  • Backend processing — photos sent for classification, CSV entries sent for refund analysis, and AI Chat prompts are processed by our backend (Supabase Edge Functions) and AI provider, then results return to your device. Photos and uploaded entries are not stored long-term in our backend.
  • Crash reports — anonymized crash details (stack traces, app version, OS version) are sent to our error-monitoring service so we can fix bugs.

4. Camera & Photo Permissions

ImportHelm requests the following device permissions only when needed:

  • Camera access — for photographing products (HTS classification), documents (OCR), and items being packed into containers (auto-fill dimensions). Camera access is only triggered when you explicitly use one of these features.
  • Photo library access — for selecting existing photos from your device for the same features. We do not browse or read your photo library outside of files you actively pick.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device's Settings. The app continues to work without camera or photo access; only the photo-driven features become unavailable.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

ImportHelm uses a small set of vendors to deliver core features. Each is listed below with the role it plays:

  • Anthropic — AI provider for the photo classifier, the in-app AI Chat, and the document OCR pipelines. Photos, prompts, and document images you submit are sent to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic does not retain submissions for training. (anthropic.com/privacy)
  • Supabase — backend infrastructure for the Edge Functions that route photos to the classifier, run the Refund Finder analysis on uploaded CSVs, and proxy AI requests. (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Sentry — crash and error monitoring. Anonymized crash reports are sent so we can fix bugs. We do not configure Sentry to send PII; only stack traces and device/app metadata. (sentry.io/privacy)
  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchases — if you subscribe to a paid tier (Pro, Team), purchase processing is handled by Apple; we do not see your payment details, only that an active subscription exists for your Apple ID. (apple.com/legal/privacy)

We do not sell or rent your information to any third party. We do not embed advertising SDKs.

6. Subscriptions & Payments

ImportHelm offers an optional paid subscription (Pro at $29.99/month, Team at $99/month) processed entirely through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We receive only your subscription status (active / canceled) and Apple's anonymous transaction identifier — never your credit card, billing address, or banking information. Subscriptions can be managed and canceled in your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Refund requests are handled by Apple per Apple's policy.

7. Children's Privacy

ImportHelm is a professional import-management tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, contact us and we will remove relevant data.

8. Your Choices & Rights

You can:

  • Delete all locally stored data via Settings > Security & Privacy > Delete All Data
  • Uninstall the app to remove all on-device data permanently
  • Revoke camera and photo permissions in your device's Settings
  • Cancel any active subscription via your Apple ID's Subscriptions page
  • Email us at the address below to request information about, or deletion of, any data we hold

Because the app is built primarily on local storage, most rights (access, deletion, portability) are exercised directly on the device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as ImportHelm adds features (e.g., cloud sync, team accounts). Changes are reflected here with a new effective date, and material changes will be surfaced inside the app.
