What changes on the right

Same data we already have. Same shipment records. Just laid out with the Liquid Glass and activity-app patterns that worked on Toolkit:

  • Greeting line + LIVE badge. "54 active, 8 arriving this week" instead of a static title. Live pulse dot says "data is current."
  • 4 vibrant status cards. Blue/Orange/Purple/Green for In Transit/At Port/Customs/Delivered. Big counts with top-edge glass shine.
  • Arrivals by Window bar chart. Visual answer to "what arrives this week vs month vs quarter." Red/orange/teal gradient bars.
  • Port Pulse grid. Today's avg dwell time per port. LA highlighted red because it's slow. Operator answers "where will my container get stuck" in two taps.
  • Shipment cards with colored left border + status pill + progress bar. Blue border for In Transit, orange for Customs, green for Delivered. Progress bar shows how far along the route they are.

Tech-feasibility: every chart is just Views + LinearGradient. Same as Dashboard. No charting library. Estimated 6-8 hours to implement end-to-end including swapping the existing ScrollView for FlatList virtualization on the shipment list.
