What changes if we go Liquid Glass

Same icons, same colors, same emoji symbols. What's different is the material:

  • Icon tiles become semi-translucent with a backdrop blur. They feel like glass over a softly colored gel instead of solid plastic.
  • Subtle highlights across the top edge of each tile add depth (a soft reflection, like real glass under light).
  • Modals + alerts get the same frosted treatment. The "Clear sample data?" confirm looks like the popup in your reference screenshot.
  • Tab bar at the bottom becomes translucent, blurred. iPhone-native feel.
  • Your colorful icon identity stays. The colors are still there, just behind a glass layer. We are not flattening or genericizing.

Tech feasibility: very doable in React Native via expo-blur on iOS. Free, on-device, no new dependencies. Android renders slightly different but acceptable. The work is about 6-10 hours to apply across the app: Helm Picks tiles, modals app-wide, tab bar, and a few headers.
