★ My recommendation: Option C (hybrid)

Option A keeps your "iPhone home-screen" feel that you said you like, but it doesn't scale well across 170 tools (every category looks the same, hard to find what you need). Option B is the cleanest modern look but loses the iPhone home-screen identity entirely.

Option C keeps the colorful tile grid for the 8 flagship tools you'd actually tap most often (the patent flow, Helm Bot, Refund Finder, etc.) and switches to a clean list for the 10 categories below. The grid is fast for muscle memory. The list is calm for browsing the long tail. You get both.
