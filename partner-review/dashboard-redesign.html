What's new on the right (and where each pattern comes from)

The redesigned Dashboard keeps every piece of data we already have but presents it the way the apps you flagged do. Specifically:

  • Greeting + Patent Pending badge in the corner. The "PRO" badge pattern from your first image. Establishes credibility instantly.
  • Time period tabs (Week / Month / Quarter / Year). The pill row from the activity apps. Lets you filter every chart and KPI in two taps.
  • 4 big colorful KPI cards with vibrant gradients. Blue / Green / Purple / Orange. Same data as today, way more visual. The top-edge reflection on each card matches our Liquid Glass aesthetic.
  • Alert banner with action CTA. The Revolut-style orange promotional banner pattern, but applied to "your CAPE window closes in 47 days, scan now."
  • Vibrant gradient bar chart. Purple-to-pink bars with the current month highlighted in orange-to-pink. Right out of your reference set.
  • Origin Exposure bars. Each country flag + horizontal gradient bar showing percentage. Visual at-a-glance answer to "what's our China exposure?"
  • Compliance Pulse with star ratings. The "Rewards Station" hierarchical card pattern, adapted for the 6 compliance pillars.

Tech-feasibility note: every chart can be built with simple React Native primitives (View + LinearGradient + flex). No charting library needed. The bar chart is just rectangles with gradient backgrounds. Estimated build: 6-10 hours total for a real implementation.

Caveat: right now the KPIs on the mockup show real numbers from your 54-shipment fixture. The actual Dashboard would pull the same data live from your imported shipments. If you have no data imported, the cards show the empty state we already have.
